Welcome to
AB Gynec & Gystro Clinic

AB Gynec and Gastro clinic has been established with the concept of providing specialised consultations and office based procedures pertaining to Gynecology and Gastro enterology.

We at our centre provide you with the best possible care by engaging in collaborative effort, providing high levels of access, communication, care quality, and safety.

Our philosophy has been to focus on patient first by providing physical and emotional comfort and striving for the best health outcome possible. We wanted to help you to achieve good health in the best possible way.

Meet Our Doctors

We have brought together India’s best doctors to bring you the best care in women’s health, high risk obstetrics & Gynec problems. Our surgeons are experts in both traditional and minimally invasive gynaecological procedures. Our team creates a warm, safe, supportive and comforting environment for patients.

Dr Bindhu Priya is a senior Consultant in Obstetrics and Gynecology and specialized in Urogynecology. She has also been trained in Melbourne, Australia and Taiwan in the fields of Urogynecology and Gynecological Laparoscopy.

Dr Amar Chand is a Senior Consultant in Surgical Gastroenterology. He obtained his Super specialization in Surgical Gastroenterology from the prestigious NIMS Hospital Hyderabad.
